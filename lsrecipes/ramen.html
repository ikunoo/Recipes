Ramen

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat-based broth,
often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and uses toppings such as sliced pork, nori, menma, and scallions.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • eggs
  • sauce
  • boulion
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Boil the water, add the seasoning packets
  2. Drop in the noodles
  3. Fan it
  4. Put an egg on it
